Our Mission

Print as an Art Form

Founded in 2012, PrintCraft began as a boutique studio with a radical belief: that print materials are a brand's most intimate touchpoint — the thing a person actually holds, feels, and keeps.

Today, we serve over 2,500 clients across 40+ countries — from startups launching their first brand to Fortune 500 companies refreshing decades-old identities. Every project receives the same meticulous attention, whether it's 50 business cards or a 10,000-piece merchandise run.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Obsessive Quality

We don't print — we craft. Every millimeter of bleed, every point of kerning, every gram of paper weight is deliberately chosen. We accept nothing less than exceptional.

02

Creative Collaboration

The best results emerge from partnership. We work alongside you — not just for you — treating every brief as a creative challenge we're mutually invested in solving.

03

Responsible Practice

Premium print doesn't have to cost the Earth. We're committed to sustainable sourcing, ethical production, and reducing our environmental impact year on year.

04

Radical Transparency

No hidden costs. No vague timelines. No surprises. We believe in clear communication, honest pricing, and keeping you informed at every production stage.

05

Continuous Innovation

We invest continuously in new print technologies, finishing techniques, and material innovations — so our clients always have access to the most cutting-edge options.

06

Client-First Mindset

Your success is our success. We measure our performance not by order volume, but by the growth, satisfaction, and loyalty of the brands we serve.

Our Journey

A Decade of Craft

2012

Studio Founded

Elena and James launch PrintCraft from a 400 sqft studio in Dubai with two Heidelberg presses and a bold vision.

2015

100th Client Milestone

Expanded team to 12, moved to larger facility, added digital and wide-format capabilities.

2018

International Expansion

Launched global shipping program and digital client portal. Won Dubai Design Week Award for Outstanding Print Excellence.

2021

Sustainable Print Initiative

Achieved carbon-neutral certification. Introduced full FSC-certified paper range and plant-based inks across all product lines.

2026

PrintCraft 2.0

Launched AI-powered artwork validation, same-day delivery in UAE, and our new online customer dashboard portal.
