Frequently Asked Questions

Minimum quantities vary by product. Business cards start at 50 units. Flyers and brochures from 100 units. Banners can be ordered individually. Merchandise (T-shirts, mugs) typically from 12–24 units per design. We can accommodate smaller runs for premium specialty prints — contact us for details.
Standard turnaround is 3–5 business days from artwork approval. We offer express 24–48 hour service for most products at a premium. Same-day printing is available in Dubai for select products. International delivery adds 2–5 business days depending on destination.
Yes! We offer full brand design and layout services. Our in-house creative team can design from scratch or adapt your existing brand assets. Design fees vary by project complexity and scope. All design work includes 2 rounds of revisions. We also offer a free artwork check service for print-ready files.
We accept PDF (preferred), AI, EPS, PSD, TIFF, and high-res PNG files. All files should be supplied in CMYK colour mode at 300dpi minimum, with 3mm bleed on all sides. Our team performs a complimentary preflight check on all submitted artwork and will advise on any issues before production begins.
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide via DHL, FedEx, and Aramex. All international shipments are fully tracked and insured. Delivery times and costs vary by destination. We offer free shipping on orders over $500 to most GCC countries. Contact us for an international shipping quote.
We stand behind every order with a 100% quality guarantee. If your order arrives damaged, with colour inaccuracies beyond tolerance, or with production defects — we will reprint and redeliver at no additional cost. We conduct rigorous quality control at every stage: pre-press, during print, and post-production.
